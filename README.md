# Features
- SE to SD Calculator
- 95% CI to SD Calculator
- Change Score SD Calculator
- Median + IQR to Mean & SD
- Median + Range to Mean & SD
- Correlation Coefficient (C.C) Calculator
- Mean Change Calculator
- SD Change Calculator
- Combine Two Groups (Cochrane Method)
- Export to Excel/CSV
- Calculate percent change between baseline and post-treatment
- Calculate Mean Post from Mean Pre and Percent Change
- Calculate Mean Post from Mean Pre and Decimal Change
- Calculate Event Rate and Percentage
- Calculate ARR from control and treatment events
- Calculate RR AND OR from control and treatment events
- Calculate NNT AND NNH from control and treatment events
- Calculate ARI, RRR, and RRI from control and treatment events
- Calculate AFE among Exposed
- Convert lipid (Cholesterol, HDL, LDL, VLDL, triglycerides)values between mmol/L and mg/dL
- Convert glucose values between mmol/L and mg/dL
- Import and export from Excel
- Dark Mode Support
## Formulas & References
- **Cochrane Handbook**: Formulas for combining groups and CI conversions.
- **Wan et al. (2014)**: Estimating the sample mean and standard deviation from the sample size, median, range and/or interquartile range.
- **Luo et al. (2018)**: Optimizing the estimator of the sample mean from the sample size, median, and range.
